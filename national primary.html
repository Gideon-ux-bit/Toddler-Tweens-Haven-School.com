Our Primary School Section
Our mission: To offer a holistic learning experience.

Our Teachers: The school recruits qualified competent and experienced Teachers. They also undergo regular training in every area of the school to maintain the high standard Toddlers&Tweens. The teachers follow the “Positive Discipline” approach when handling learners which is being kind and yet firm.

Specialist teachers work in the Library, IT, and co- curricular activities like Sports and Swimming.

Time:
P1 – P3: 8:00am – 4:00pm
Facilities: The school has modern adequate and well equipped facilities for all subjects and school activities. The classrooms are tastefully decorated with all the amenities to suit the Primary learning. An Audio Visual room, Computer and IT room, a well stocked Library, a Sick Bay, and a Game Area. We also have clean separate washrooms for Girls and Boys. We have installed Close Circuit TV in all areas of the school to ensure security.

The learners of Toddlers&Tweens Haven School are GEMS. The school has Four Houses developing a competitive spirit among the learners to become higher achievers in all activities. We also believe the children will reflect the qualities of the gems as in:

Topaz, being the yellow, house will stimulate the children to reflect sunshine.
Ruby, being the red house, aims at producing dynamic and stable achievers
Emerald, the green house, aims at developing kind and compassionate winners
Saphire, the blue house, aims at reflecting amazing wisdom and honest achievers.

These houses blend to become The Hungry Caterpillar School, a Treasure house of precious gems.
